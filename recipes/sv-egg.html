The Froached egg- the perfect compromise!

This'll change the first 10 min of your day for the rest of life. I just set the bar, now play along.

Ingredients:

  • A dozen eggs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Bacon
  • Brown Sugar
  • Salt and Pepper

Egg Steps:

  1. Cook eggs at 167F for 12 min
  2. Drop into ice bath for 30 min or overnight
  3. Break egg into nonstick pan with little bit of vegetable oil
  4. Set heat to medium and fry.
  5. Add breadcrumbs and oil to top side.
  6. flip with with two spatulas as egg is fragile
  7. Practice makes perfect, so rinse and repeat!

Bacon Marmalade Steps:

  1. Chop bacon into bits
  2. Throw into pan with an oil without too much fat and heat
  3. Strain
  4. Add back to pan with tbsp or 2 of brwon sugar and a splash of water.
  5. Set to low heat and watch water evaporate
  6. Serve egg and bacon together, on top of toast or a bagel.
