Ramen- Do it right!

To say me and ramen have history is an understatement, last time I had a noodle brick phase I was suprised to sit down and feel a burning spear clogging my intestines and protruding out my ass and right thru the seat. This is also known as a hemmorhoid.

Don't overdue the brick, kids. Obviously, as a student in the jaws of poverty, I couldn't live wihout ramen for very long. Let's do it right this time around.

Ingredients:

  • Skinless pork belly
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirim
  • Shallots
  • Garlic cloves
  • Fresh korean noodles
  • Mushrooms

Steps:

  1. Cut pork belly in half, set each next to each other, and wack it with a spatula until its flattened
  2. Season with a mix of salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper
  3. Roll it up like a rug, tie it with butcher twine
  4. "Use the “water immersion” method. Place the food in a zipper lock bag and slowly lower the bagged food into a bowl of water, letting the pressure of the water press air through the top of the bag. Once most of the air is out of the bag, carefully seal it just above the water line."
  5. While I've heard that pigs fly, I was suprised to find they float too. Weigh down the pork belly bag by adding 2 butter knifes.
  6. Heat for 8 hours at 160F
  7. Strain and save the pork belly juices for broth.
  8. Put pork belly in an ice bath for a few minutes
  9. Boil eggs for 6 min
  10. Put eggs in an ice bath for a few min too
  11. Mix soy sauce, mirim, and water into a pan
  12. Drop eggs into this sauce and marinate for 2 hours
  13. Make broth in a pot. Pour pork belly juice into pan with garlic cloves and shallots. Then fill the pot up with water and set on high heat for 30 min.
  14. Season with salt and pepper.
  15. spoon test for flavor until you're ready to strain it, then package it for fridge
  16. Follow the directions on your noodle bag, so boil for 3 minutes and cook some mushrooms too if you'd like.
  17. Sear pork belly on skillet with butter and high heat. Each side for 2 min with a spatula
  18. Add marinated, sliced eggs, noodles, broth, and meat together for the perfect ramen bowl.
